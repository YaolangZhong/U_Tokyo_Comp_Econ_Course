## Computational Economics Papers

### AKM_2022_IER - Deep Equilibrium Nets
**Authors**: Marlon Azinovic, Domonkos Kondor, Thomas M. Mertens  
**Journal**: International Economic Review, 2022

Deep neural networks for solving DSGE models. Combines NN function approximation with equilibrium conditions.

### GTT_2023_QE - Pareto Extrapolation
**Authors**: Émilien Gouin-Bonenfant, Alexis Akira Toda  
**Journal**: Quantitative Economics, 2023

Analytical framework for tail behavior of wealth distributions in Bewley-Huggett-Aiyagari models.

### Rust_1997_EMTA - Randomization for DP
**Author**: John Rust  
**Journal**: Econometrica, 1997

Foundational paper on randomization to break the curse of dimensionality in dynamic programming.

### MM_2014_JEDC - Numerical Methods Survey
**Authors**: Lilia Maliar, Serguei Maliar  
**Source**: Handbook of Computational Economics, 2014

Survey of numerical methods for large-scale dynamic economic models.

### MMW_2021_JME - Deep Learning for Economics
**Authors**: Lilia Maliar, Serguei Maliar, Pablo Winant  
**Journal**: Journal of Monetary Economics, 2021

Deep learning for solving dynamic economic models with high-dimensional state spaces.

## Trade Economics

### Melitz_2003_EMTA - Trade and Productivity
**Author**: Marc J. Melitz  
**Journal**: Econometrica, 2003

Seminal paper on heterogeneous firms and trade. Shows how trade liberalization reallocates resources toward more productive firms.

## Reinforcement Learning Foundations

### Sutton_1988_ML - Temporal Difference Learning
**Author**: Richard S. Sutton  
**Journal**: Machine Learning, 1988

Introduces TD learning, bridging Monte Carlo and dynamic programming methods.

---

## Deep Reinforcement Learning

### LHPHTS_2016_ICLR_DDPG - Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient
**Authors**: Lillicrap, Hunt, Pritzel, Heess, Erez, Tassa, Silver, Wierstra  
**Venue**: ICLR, 2016

DDPG: Actor-critic for continuous control. Combines DQN ideas with deterministic policy gradients.

### MBMRLPHVK_2016_ICML_A3C - Asynchronous Actor-Critic
**Authors**: Mnih, Badia, Mirza, Graves, Lillicrap, Harley, Silver, Kavukcuoglu  
**Venue**: ICML, 2016

A3C: Asynchronous advantage actor-critic. Parallel training for stability and speed.

### SWDRKA_2017_arXiv_PPO - Proximal Policy Optimization
**Authors**: Schulman, Wolski, Dhariwal, Radford, Klimov  
**Source**: arXiv, 2017

PPO: Simple, stable policy gradient method. Clips objective to prevent large policy updates.

### HZAL_2018_ICML_SAC - Soft Actor-Critic
**Authors**: Haarnoja, Zhou, Abbeel, Levine  
**Venue**: ICML, 2018

SAC: Maximum entropy RL. Balances exploration and exploitation via entropy regularization.
